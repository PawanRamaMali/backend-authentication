Senior Development Engineer

About the Employer
  • Manx Telecom
  • Isle of Man
  • Featured Employer
Annual Salary
£30k - £50k

Job Description

If you thought Manx Telecom were exclusively concerned with the exceptional proposition they offer to residents of the Isle of Man, you’ll be surprised to see the scale of their international Global Solutions business. With a truly global footprint, offering cutting edge technology in exciting markets, we are punching well above our weight. If terms such as MVNO/E, IOT, M2M, interconnect, CAMEL, Sigtran & Diameter feature in your specialist subject – this is a role worth noting. Being a small team with a wide scope there is nowhere to hide – you will be involved in all aspects of the development of our network capabilities.

About the Role

Working as part of the Mobile & Fixed Development Team developing and implementing new or enhanced network services, capacity expansion or upgrades into the Mobile network with focus on our Global Solutions capabilities. A Senior Development Engineer is able to take the lead on project tasks of design and implementation with minimal supervision. Also expected to support the Operations teams as required for fault or incident escalations. A Senior Development Engineer is expected to have specialist knowledge in more than one area of the domain.

About the Ideal Candidate

As a thoroughly engaged member of the team, working closely with others and collaborating to deliver exceptional levels of service – you’ll also have unrivalled technical expertise, illustrated with:

  • Essential - Strong core mobile network experience (both voice & data ideally but will accept one)
  • Essential - Knowledge of signalling systems and network analysers
  • Essential - Good IP design skills
  • Preferred - a background in M2M/ IOT environments
  • Self-motivate with ability to work independently at most times as well as collaboratively as part of a cohesive team.
  • Great written and verbal skills and able to communicate up to Head of Networks, both technical and non-technical.
  • Ability to listen and capture requirements accurately
  • Ability to coach and mentor effectively more junior engineers
  • Flexibility and ability to work well under pressure.
  • Analytical, evaluative and problem-solving abilities

In return, we can offer you a highly competitive salary, with:

  • Performance related reward
  • Company matched Pension Scheme
  • Fully subsidised private Healthcare - BUPA
  • Annual Bonus Scheme
  • Excellent Annual Leave entitlements
  • Corporate Discounts and job-related benefits
  • Company Health screening program
  • Outstanding training and development opportunities
  • Free Onsite parking
