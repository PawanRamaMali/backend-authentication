Senior Software Engineer

About the Employer
  • Microgaming
  • Isle of Man
  • Featured Employer
Annual Salary
£30k - £50k

Job Description

Do you have strong application management experience and a solid understanding of software development and deployment? We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our team on a 12 month fixed-term contract, providing operational system support, business analysis, systems deployment, and training for our customers. Working closely with teams across the business, you will put your extensive skills to good use in analysing, designing, implementing and supporting a range of enterprise applications, including Dynamics CRM, SharePoint, Sitecore, ADAM, and Remedy, PHP and .NET applications. You will be responsible for Live Support adhering to ITL Standards, Live Deployments, management of 3rd Parties and mentoring of Junior team members. If your expertise in working with complex processes and systems is matched by a passion for providing innovative solutions, this could be the ideal role for you. In return for your outstanding development skills, we can offer you a generous benefits package, including private health cover, a daily allowance for our canteen and coffee shop, free access to our onsite gym, and tailored career development opportunities. A leading supplier of online gaming content to operators worldwide, Microgaming fosters a diverse and innovative culture whose people are empowered to be competitive, adventurous, creative and agile..

What you need to succeed

  • 5+ years’ experience in a software development/application management environment
  • Project management experience is desirable
  • An understanding of server hardware and configuration
  • Experience in front/back-end development
  • Knowledge of and experience in Sitecore, MS SharePoint, SQL, MS Dynamics CRM, BMC Remedy, WordPress, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
